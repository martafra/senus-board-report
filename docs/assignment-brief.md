## Reference Materials

Senus PLC is an Assiduous client. The Irish headquartered provider of Natural Capital management
software and technology solutions was admitted to trading on the Euronext Access market in Dublin on
22 December 2025.

Founded by Brendan Allen, Eoghan Finneran and Joe Desbonnet in 2017 and headquartered in Ireland, after
8 years of R&D and implementing projects with marquee clients, Senus is seeking to establish itself as a
leading global provider of Natural Capital management software and technology solutions for Governments,
State agencies, financial institutions, corporations, farmers and other landowners. Natural Capital
comprises the world's stock of natural resources including soil, air, water and habitats which underpin
economies and society. Senus enables clients to optimise the use and preserve the value of their nature
related assets.

Senus' proprietary Natural Capital management software and technology solutions enable the
'Measurement', 'Reporting,' 'Verification' and 'Planning' of value chains associated with the use of
natural resources in productive industry. Senus' software and technology solutions are licenced directly
to corporate and government clients, or through intermediaries such as agronomists or other consultants
to end users. Senus transforms complex environmental data into actionable insights enabling clients to
proactively manage their Natural Capital to unlock productivity gains and reduce environmental and
operational risks.

Senus is currently providing its solutions to clients in Ireland, increasingly in the UK, and has an
initial presence in mainland Europe. For the year ended 30 June 2025, Senus recorded revenue of
€836,991, serving 138 customer accounts. Senus reported its first set of results as a public company,
reporting its half year results for the period ended December 2025 in March 2026. Under its strategy
Senus 2030, the company is planning to grow sales at an annual compound growth rate of no less than 50%
through the period 2026 - 2030.

All the reference information required is available on the Senus investor website:
https://app.assiduous.tech/investor-relations/senus

## Project Instructions

By reference to the materials available on the Senus investor relations website, and your understanding
of modern software engineering, AI-native development and corporate finance, please complete the
following project:

### 1. Senus PLC 'Board Report'

- Using the historic financial information available, design and build an AI-native platform that
  prepares a Board Report for Management, the Board, Equity Investors and Credit Providers to analyse
  and understand the performance of Senus PLC.
- Your solution should demonstrate strong software engineering, product thinking and technical
  decision-making. Select appropriate financial metrics and present them through an intuitive,
  interactive user experience. For example, include:
  - Growth & Revenue (YoY, MoM, Customers, Channels, Bookings etc.)
  - Profitability (Gross Margin, Operating Margin, EBITDA Margin, Cost Breakdown etc.)
  - Cash & Liquidity (EBITDA to Free Cash Flow Bridge, Cash Runway, Working Capital etc.)
  - Solvency & Leverage (Debt Service Coverage Ratios etc.)
  - Returns (ROCE etc.)
  - AI-powered insights, commentary or financial analysis where appropriate.

Note: You may use any and all AI tools that you feel are appropriate for the tasks. You must be able to
detail the steps you took, tools you used and stand over the outputs generated.

## Technical Deliverables

Please prepare the deliverables below, and submit to Technology Graduate Submission:

- Deploy it using a full stack web application that is interactive and representative of a platform a
  CEO would log in to and use. Focus on AI methods for extracting financial information from the source
  documents into a database powering a model that underpins the Board Report application.
- Demonstrate production-quality engineering practices, including thoughtful system architecture,
  scalable application design, modern frontend development, backend APIs, data modelling, AI
  integration and a polished user experience.
- We are interested in your technical decisions as much as the final product. Include a brief README
  outlining your architecture, technologies used, AI-assisted development workflow, assumptions made
  and how you validated the outputs.
- Provide the following deliverables:
  1. Youtube link to a demo
  2. GitHub link to project repo
  3. One page write up (optional)
